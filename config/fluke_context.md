# Fluke Corporation - Competitive Intelligence Context

## Company Overview

Fluke Corporation is a world leader in the manufacture, distribution, and service of electronic test tools, biomedical equipment, and networking solutions. Founded in 1948 and headquartered in Everett, Washington, Fluke is part of Fortive Corporation.

## Mission
To provide the world's best, most reliable test and measurement equipment that helps professionals keep their world up and running.

## Key Product Categories

### 1. Digital Multimeters (DMMs)
- **Flagship Products**: Fluke 87V, Fluke 117, Fluke 179
- **Market Position**: Industry leader with 40%+ market share
- **Key Differentiators**: 
  - CAT IV safety ratings
  - True-RMS measurements
  - Industrial-grade durability
  - Lifetime warranty on select models

### 2. Clamp Meters
- **Flagship Products**: Fluke 376 FC, Fluke 323
- **Key Differentiators**:
  - iFlex flexible current probe technology
  - Fluke Connect wireless capability
  - Wide measurement ranges

### 3. Thermal Imaging Cameras
- **Flagship Products**: Fluke Ti450, TiS60+
- **Market Position**: Growing segment, competing with FLIR
- **Key Differentiators**:
  - LaserSharp Auto Focus
  - IR-Fusion technology
  - Rugged design for field use

### 4. Oscilloscopes
- **Flagship Products**: ScopeMeter series, Fluke 190 Series
- **Key Differentiators**:
  - Portable, battery-powered operation
  - Industrial ratings (IP51, CAT III/IV)
  - Combined DMM and oscilloscope functionality

### 5. Power Quality Analyzers
- **Flagship Products**: Fluke 1760, 435-II
- **Key Differentiators**:
  - EN50160 compliance
  - GPS time synchronization
  - Energy loss monetization

### 6. Process Calibration Tools
- **Flagship Products**: Fluke 754, Fluke 726
- **Key Differentiators**:
  - HART communication
  - Documenting process calibrators
  - Automated procedures

### 7. Insulation Testers
- **Flagship Products**: Fluke 1555, 1587 FC
- **Key Differentiators**:
  - High voltage testing up to 10kV
  - Polarization Index testing
  - Fluke Connect integration

## Pricing Strategy

- **Premium Positioning**: Fluke products typically command 20-40% premium over competitors
- **Value Justification**: Total cost of ownership, reliability, safety
- **Tiered Offerings**: Good/Better/Best product lines
- **Professional Focus**: Not competing on low-end consumer market
